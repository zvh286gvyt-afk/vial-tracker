How to Use Vial Tracker

Adding a vitamin

Tap the + button (bottom right). Enter the name, how many days it lasts before expiring (defaults to 28), how many vials you're starting with, and the date you received them.

Using vials

Tap Use 1 on a vitamin's card each time you administer one. It subtracts from your oldest stock first and logs it in the usage report.

Restocking

Tap + Restock when new vials arrive. Enter how many and the date received — the app calculates the new expiration automatically.

Status colors

OK plenty of time left  ·  Order more soon expires within 7 days  ·  Expired past the expiration date

Notification icons (top right)

🔔 Reminders are off — tap to turn them on.

🔔✓ Reminders are on. You'll get a push notification 7 days before any vial expires, so you know it's time to order more.

🔕 Notifications are blocked in your phone/browser settings. Enable them in Settings, then reload the app.

Usage report

Tap 📊 to see how many vials of each vitamin were used this week, month, or year. Reset Report permanently clears that history — it will always ask you to confirm first, since it can't be undone.

Deleting a vitamin

Tap Delete on a card to remove that vitamin and all its stock records. This also asks for confirmation first.
